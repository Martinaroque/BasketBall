Why Basketball is my favorite sport

Basketball is my favorite sport because I played it for 13 years in the federation. I am twice national champion in under-19, I am also national champion in the second division of seniors and I have a few more medals from championships that I participated. At the age of 13 I started playing basketball and fell in love with the sport, it is a very complete sport, both physically and mentally/emotional. It is a game that is played in 4 parts of 10 minutes with 5 players on each side.
